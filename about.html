Hey what's up!

I'm a growing developer interested in tech and entrepreneurship, currently studying Computer Science at Duke University. I was born and raised in Shenzhen, China, a city that has now grown into the Silicon Valley of China. At the age of 13, however, I took a risk to move across the globe by myself and attend a private high school in Southborough, Massachusetts. My experiences have taught me to seek comfort in discomfort and build a strong sense of self. As an early and avid adopter of technologies, I'm fascinated by how they make their way into our daily lives. I love trying to explain complex concepts in very simple terms that even my 11-year-old brother can understand.
