Mi.me

The Reverse Hackathon hosted by Hack Mental Health is a one day event that brings together academics, designers, ethicists, computer scientists, psychologists, entrepreneurs, and educators to redesign tech tools in a more meaningful, socially responsible way. The final product was a 5 minute product proposal to humanely redesign an existing technology. Our team was nominated one of top 10 teams to compete in the final round of presentations. Check out the Devpost Story for more details.

  • Role: Front-end Design, User Flow
  • Technologies: Sketch, Framer
  • Date: June 2018
  • Brief: A tool that uses mindful interjections to increase awareness and control of time spent on social media apps.
